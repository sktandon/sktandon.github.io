Introduction

A token extrovert, Sara Tandon is a fiend for communication, informs in an engaging way, and brings a little joy wherever she goes. Sara's first love is traveling, having lived in 4 countries, visited more than 55 countries, and all 7 continents. Years of international volunteer work and a multi-cultural upbringing, instilled an empathetic heart and inquisitive mind in Sara.

Sara is currently working toward a PhD in Human Computer Interaction from King's College London. Always in pursuit to sharpen her communication skills, Sara focused her thesis on cognitive ability and its impact on perception of data visualization. Sara also holds a MSc in Data Science from KCL, and a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science from Pepperdine University (USA).
